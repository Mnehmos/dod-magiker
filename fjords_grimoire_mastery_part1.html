/// PNEUMA ///
The Art of Mind and Spirit

Pneuma is the realm of consciousness, thought, and the entities that exist beyond flesh. It's reading minds, commanding spirits, reaching into the invisible world that overlaps our own. It's also the most intimate Art‚Äîwhen you touch someone's mind, you bring a piece of them back with you. I still have fragments of thoughts from people I've read. Their fears. Their desires. Their secrets.

Pneuma work requires ethical boundaries you must set for yourself, because the Art itself has none.

METHODOLOGY: I focus on the target while holding a clear quartz to my forehead (third eye position, naturally). I speak the Opening Phrase and their surface thoughts become audible‚Äînot with my ears, but in my mind. Sounds like they're whispering directly into my skull.

/// VITA ///

Vita is the manipulation of living tissue, biological processes, the code of life itself. Healing and harming, transformation and enhancement, the boundary between life and death.

Working with Vita means understanding that you're not dealing with inert matter‚Äîliving things WANT things. They fight back. Your own body will resist your attempts to change it. And when you resurrect the dead, sometimes what comes back isn't quite what left.
