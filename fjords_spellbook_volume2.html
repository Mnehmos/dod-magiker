High Ritual Practice
On the Nature of Modular Magic:

After years of rigid adherence to formulae, I've come to understand something the old grimoires never quite articulated: there are many paths to the same destination. A lock may be picked (Alchemy), the key stolen from someone's mind (Pneuma), or the door itself convinced it was never locked (Vita + Pneuma on the door's "memory").

This volume documents that revelation. Each spell here is less a specific formula and more a concept—a problem with multiple solutions. I present my preferred High Ritual methodology, but I've noted alternatives. Some Folk practitioner will probably read this and scoff at my elaborate circles when they could achieve the same with chicken bones and spit. Let them. I'll be over here with my precise geometric accuracy, thank you.

The Arts are tools. The Will is the craftsman. Don't let anyone tell you there's only one way to build a cathedral—or tear one down.

— F.T.H.

The Witness Veil
EXPERTISE 1 MODULAR CONCEALMENT DR 12-14
Make yourself forgettable, unnoticeable, or literally invisible. The fundamental concealment working every practitioner needs. I use this constantly—can't study Reality Scars if security guards keep asking what I'm doing in the condemned warehouse at 3 AM.
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: I inscribe my name on a black mirror in silver, then systematically erase each letter while reciting a formula of diminishment. As each letter vanishes, I become less "present" to observers. Complete erasure equals invisibility. The mirror must be kept on my person for the duration.
⚙ Modular Applications
PNEUMA APPROACH: "Notice-Me-Not"
DR 12 | Affect minds to simply not register your presence. You're there, they just don't care. Presence test to resist if you do something dramatic.
My Notes: Most energy-efficient. People's eyes slide right over you. I once stood in an elevator with a Man's agent for seven floors.
PYR + ALCHEMY: "Light-Bending"
DR 14 | Bend light around yourself using both Arts. True invisibility, but requires constant concentration and doesn't work on thermal imaging.
My Notes: Flashy but exhausting. Good for short-term applications. That +2 DR for the extra Art hurts.
CHORA: "Spatial Displacement"
DR 12 | You're slightly "beside" where you appear to be. Observers see empty space.
My Notes: Weird side effects. Had a conversation with someone while using this once—they kept turning to where they THOUGHT I was. Disorienting for everyone involved.
VITA: "Camouflage Skin"
DR 12 | Your skin and clothes adapt to surroundings like a chameleon. Must stay still or move slowly.
My Notes: Creepy. Your body literally changes color. The first time I used this, I spent ten minutes afterwards making sure I'd returned to my original skin tone.
⚙ Base Mechanics
Approach Arts Required DR Duration
Pneuma (Mental) Pneuma +1 12 Until dawn or noticed
Pyr + Alchemy Pyr +1, Alchemy +1 14 Concentration required
Chora (Space) Chora +1 12 Until dawn
Vita (Biological) Vita +1 12 While stationary/slow
Universal Components: Method-dependent, but all require focus item
Backlash Risk: Minor to Moderate depending on approach
The Message
EXPERTISE 1 MODULAR COMMUNICATION DR 10-16
Send a message to someone far away. Crucial for coordinating with my network of informants about Reality Scar sightings without triggering Surveillance State (fucking smartphones are basically tracking devices for the Man).
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: I write the message on rice paper with ink made from my blood and the recipient's (I keep samples). I burn the paper while speaking their name thrice, and the message manifests as ash-writing that appears on the nearest flat surface to them.
⚙ Modular Applications
PNEUMA: "Thought Whisper"
DR 12 | Message appears directly in their mind as a thought in your voice.
My Notes: Intimate and immediate. Can be unsettling for the recipient. One contact thought they were going mad until I explained.
CHORA: "Folded Words"
DR 14 | Speak into folded space; your words emerge from thin air near recipient.
My Notes: Dramatic but requires knowing their exact location. The +2 DR for Chora when you don't have line of sight makes this challenging.
VITA: "Carrier Bird"
DR 10 | Enchant an actual bird to carry your message. Old school.
My Notes: Slower but charming. Plus the bird can bring back a reply. I have a raven I use regularly. Named him Nevermore. I'm hilarious.
PYR: "Smoke Signal 2.0"
DR 12 | Message written in smoke visible only to recipient.
My Notes: Requires them to be watching sky/nearby smoke source. Good for pre-arranged signals.
CHRONOS: "Time-Delayed Message"
DR 16 | Send message that arrives at specific future time.
My Notes: Useful for alibis. "I was sending that message when the incident occurred, officer."
⚙ Base Mechanics
Range: Usually unlimited with proper sympathetic link
Message Length: ~25 words for basic casting, longer costs +2 DR per 25 words
Components: Sympathetic link to recipient (hair, blood, true name, etc.)
Backlash Risk: Low, but messages can be intercepted by other practitioners
The Seal
EXPERTISE 1 MODULAR SECURITY DR 12-14
Lock or unlock doors, containers, files—anything that can be secured. Essential for both breaking into places that house Reality Scar information and protecting my own Sanctuary from nosy visitors.
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: For locking: I draw a complex sigil over the lock mechanism in blessed oil, then speak a word of binding in Enochian. For unlocking: I draw the mirror-image sigil and speak the word backwards. Simple, elegant, effective.
⚙ Modular Applications
ALCHEMY: "Molecular Fusion/Separation"
DR 12 | Literally fuse the lock shut or separate the mechanism's components.
My Notes: Permanent until reversed. I locked a door so thoroughly once that we had to break the wall. Oops.
PNEUMA: "Mind the Lock"
DR 14 | Convince the lock it's already open/closed. Works on electronic locks too.
My Notes: Yes, locks have a form of awareness. Everything does if you dig deep enough into Pneuma theory. It's disturbing.
VITA: "Grown Shut"
DR 12 | For wooden doors—grow the wood across the frame. Organic locks only.
My Notes: Takes a minute to work. Good for barricading yourself somewhere. Less good when you need a quick exit.
CHORA: "Keyhole to Nowhere"
DR 14 | Lock opens to a dimension where the key doesn't exist. Unopenable until dispelled.
My Notes: Conceptually elegant. Practically a pain when I forget which doors I've done this to.
PYR: "Welded Shut"
DR 12 | Superheat metal locks to weld them. Loud, obvious, permanent.
My Notes: Not subtle. Use only when subtlety is already compromised.
⚙ Base Mechanics
Complexity Modifier: Simple locks DR 12, Complex locks +2 DR, Electronic locks +4 DR
Duration: Permanent until dispelled (except Vita method)
Range: Touch usually, or line of sight with +2 DR
Backlash Risk: Minimal, unless you lock yourself IN somewhere
Object Reading

Learn the history of an object by touching it. Invaluable when examining Reality Scar fragments—I need to know where they came from, what event created them, what they've been exposed to. Also excellent for finding out if that "virgin" ritual component has actually been used before.
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: I hold the object while in a meditative trance, having fasted for 12 hours and positioned myself in a circle of white sand. I recite the Litany of Echoes and let the object's accumulated experiences flow into me. It's like watching a very badly edited film from the object's perspective.
⚙ Modular Applications
CHRONOS: "Temporal Echo Reading"
DR 14 | See the object's timeline directly. Most reliable method.
My Notes: Clean, accurate, gives dates. My preferred method for anything important.
PNEUMA: "Psychometric Touch"
DR 12 | Feel the emotions and intentions of everyone who's touched it.
My Notes: More subjective but faster. Good for detecting curses or malicious intent.
ALCHEMY: "Atomic Memory"
DR 16 | Read the object's history from its molecular structure.
My Notes: Incredibly detailed but takes forever to interpret. I see EVERYTHING down to temperature changes.
VITA (if formerly living): "Death Memory"
DR 12 | If object is bone, wood, leather—learn about the creature it came from.

⚙ Base Mechanics
Information Depth: Recent events easy, ancient history +2 DR per century
Duration: Vision lasts d6 minutes
Clarity: Depends on approach and object's "history saturation"
Backlash Risk: Moderate—can experience object's traumas directly
⚠ Psychological Warning:
Never use this on weapons that have killed people unless you're prepared for nightmares. Found that out reading a Reality Scar fragment that turned out to be from a collapsed psy-ops facility. Saw things. Still see things.
The Compass
EXPERTISE 1 MODULAR NAVIGATION DR 12-16
Find something you're looking for. Person, place, object—doesn't matter. The more specific your target, the easier the spell. "Find Bob" is easier than "Find someone who knows about Reality Scars." This is different from the Scavenger's Compass in my staff—that's specifically attuned to Reality Scars. This is general-purpose.
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: I use a pendulum made from crystal suspended over a map (or for nearby targets, just over the area). I charge it with my intention while reciting the Formula of Seeking, and it swings toward the target. More precise than GPS and doesn't trigger Surveillance State.
⚙ Modular Applications
CHORA: "Spatial Dowsing"
DR 12 | Follow the "pull" of space folding toward target.
My Notes: Physical sensation, like being tugged. Reliable for nearby targets.
PNEUMA: "Sympathetic Resonance"
DR 14 | If you have a connection to target, feel where they are.
My Notes: Requires personal item or blood. Incredibly accurate. Also incredibly creepy to the target if they're sensitive.
CHRONOS: "Probability Pathfinding"
DR 16 | Calculate most probable location based on temporal patterns.
My Notes: Complex but useful for finding mobile targets. "Where will Bob be in 3 hours?"
VITA: "Tracker Beast"
DR 10 | Enchant a dog/rat/bird to lead you to target.
My Notes: Only works if target has scent trail or physical trail to follow. Very reliable once established.
PYR: "Heat Seeking"
DR 12 | If looking for person/living thing, follow their heat signature.
My Notes: Limited range but excellent for urban tracking. Everyone's thermal signature is unique.
⚙ Base Mechanics
Target Specificity: Named individual DR 12, "type of person" DR 14, "anything like X" DR 16
Range: Method dependent, usually 1-5 miles
Duration: Until target found or dawn
Components: Sympathetic link helpful but not required
Backlash Risk: Low, but can lead you to wrong target if you fumble
Minor Mending
EXPERTISE 1 MODULAR REPAIR DR 10-14
Fix broken things. Tools break when collecting Reality Scar fragments—they're not designed for the stress. My clothes get torn climbing through condemned buildings. My glasses shattered when a demon looked at me funny. This spell has saved me thousands in replacement costs.
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: I arrange the broken pieces in their original configuration, trace the cracks with gold leaf, and speak the Formula of Restoration. The gold melts into the cracks and bonds the pieces. The repair is visible but stronger than original.
⚙ Modular Applications
ALCHEMY: "Molecular Rebonding"
DR 10 | Simply fuse the molecules back together. Clean, invisible repair.
My Notes: Best for glass, metal, ceramics. Doesn't work on complex mechanisms without increasing DR.
CHRONOS: "Temporal Restoration"
DR 14 | Rewind the object to before it broke. Perfect repair.
My Notes: Elegant but draining. The object "remembers" being broken and wants to return to that state.
VITA: "Organic Regrowth"
DR 10 | For wood, bone, leather—make it regrow the missing part.
My Notes: Takes time. Good for overnight repairs. The regrown part is living tissue for a while, which is weird.
PNEUMA: "Convince It It's Not Broken"
DR 12 | Object believes it's whole. Functions normally but is technically still broken.
My Notes: Temporary fix. Lasts until dawn or until examined closely. Good for emergencies.
PYR: "Heat Welding"
DR 10 | Melt and reform broken edges. Crude but effective for metal.
My Notes: Leaves obvious repair marks. Functional, not pretty.
⚙ Base Mechanics
Complexity: Simple break DR 10, Complex mechanism +2 DR, Shattered/multiple pieces +4 DR
Size Limit: Can repair up to large item (door-sized), bigger items +2 DR per size category
Duration: Permanent (except Pneuma method)
Backlash Risk: Minimal. Worst case: repair is weak and fails under stress
The Warding
EXPERTISE 1 MODULAR PROTECTION DR 12-14

I ward against bullets when meeting informants, against poison when dealing with Folk practitioners I don't trust, against surveillance when I don't want to be tracked.
MY HIGH RITUAL METHOD: I inscribe a sigil representing the threat on my skin with ink mixed with something representing safety from that threat (iron filings for bullets, antitoxin for poison, etc.). I activate it with a word of denial. The sigil glows faintly when the ward is triggered.
⚙ Modular Applications
ALCHEMY: "Substance Rejection"
DR 12 | One type of physical substance can't touch you (bullets, acid, water, etc.)
My Notes: Very specific. "Bullets" works. "Harm" doesn't. Need to define the substance clearly.
PNEUMA: "Mental Ward"

My Notes: Essential when dealing with other Pneuma practitioners. Like psychic armor.
VITA: "Biological Immunity"
DR 10 | Immune to one disease, poison, or biological agent for duration.
My Notes: Great for exploring contaminated Reality Scars. I warded against radiation once. Still felt sick but didn't die.
PYR: "Energy Deflection"
DR 14 | Deflect one type of energy (heat, electricity, radiation, kinetic force)
My Notes: Saved my life in the Tesla factory. Electricity just... curved around me.
CHRONOS: "Temporal Dodge"
DR 14 | You're slightly "ahead" in time of one type of attack, so it misses.
My Notes: Conceptually weird but incredibly effective. The attack happens "when you used to be."
⚙ Base Mechanics
Specificity: Must name exactly what you're warding against
Strength: Basic ward blocks minor to moderate instances, +2 DR for strong protection
Duration: Until dawn or until ward triggered 3 times
Limitation: ONE threat type only per casting
Backlash Risk: Moderate—failed ward can actually attract what you're warding against

/// Volume II: Closing Thoughts ///
On Versatility Over Specialization:

These fifteen spells represent what I consider the essential toolkit for a Reality Scar hunter who practices High Ritual. But here's the thing—they're templates, not commandments.

I've shown you how I do it: elaborate circles, precise timing, expensive components, hours of preparation. Some Folk practitioner is probably reading this and thinking "I could do that with a chicken bone and some spit in five minutes." And you know what? They're probably right. Their version would be messier, less predictable, more likely to explode in their face—but it would work.

The Arts don't care about your methodology. They care about your Will and your knowledge. The ritual forms are just focusing mechanisms. If you can achieve the same mental state with drums and dancing that I achieve with geometric precision, more power to you. We all end up in the same place: trying to tear a hole in consensus reality large enough to matter.
