What Is Död Magiker?

Död Magiker is a tabletop role-playing game about magical rebellion in a world controlled by institutional power. You are a Practitioner—someone who has discovered the truth that reality is malleable, and you're ready to fight back against "the Man."

In this world, magic users have been systematically oppressed by governments, corporations, and academia working together to maintain control over reality itself. The old gods are dead, killed by jealous mortals who now rule through lies and artificial limitations.

But you know better. You understand that the Crown of Reality belongs to those bold enough to claim it. Through your mastery of the Arts—Alchemy, Chora, Chronos, Pneuma, Pyr, and Vita—you can reshape the world as it should be.

Warning: Player characters cannot be allied in any way, shape, or form with the Man or any of their puppets. This is the one rule that cannot be changed.

The Rules Are Made To Be Broken

Död Magiker uses a simple d20 system with six attributes and six magical Arts. Magic is dangerous, unpredictable, and incredibly powerful—exactly as it should be.

Tests

Roll d20 + Attribute vs. Difficulty Rating (DR 12 standard, 14 hard, 18 nearly impossible)

Magic

Describe what you want to do, explain how you do it, then test the appropriate Art

Consequences

Failed magic brings Backlash or Curses. Success brings power beyond imagination

The Man

Surveillance State, Social Stigma, and institutional opposition at every turn

Choose Your Practice

How do you work magic? Your Practice determines your methodology, strengths, and weaknesses:

High Ritual

Elaborate ceremonies, ancient languages, stacks of books. Adaptable and versatile, but takes time to prepare.

Folk

Traditional beliefs, herbalism, superstitions. Connected to the natural world with quasi-magical gifts.

Sacramental

Organized religion, prayer, congregation power. Draw strength from the faithful.

Chaos

Ecstatic trances, altered states, shattered perceptions. The most rebellious of all Practices.

Artificer

Technology and tools, mad science, engineering wonders. Often becomes part of the Man.

Ascetic

Mind over body, meditation, deprivation. Unbreakable will and iron control.
